OAuth Demo

Demonstration of integrating the OAuth 2.0 standard with popular providers to enable quick & secure account management.

Get Started

You are not signed in yet, so begin by clicking your favourite provider listed below!

  • Discord

You will be redirected to the provider's website, then prompted to grant this website access your account's information.

The prompt on the provider's website should inform you about the specific account information we are requesting.
