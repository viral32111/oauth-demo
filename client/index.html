OAuth Demo

Demonstration of integrating the OAuth 2.0 standard with popular providers to enable quick & secure account management.

Get Started

You are not signed in yet, so begin by signing in with your favourite provider below.

You will be sent to their website & prompted to grant access your account's information.

Tip: Hover over a provider to see what user information this website is requesting.

  • Google
  • Microsoft
  • Apple
  • Twitter
